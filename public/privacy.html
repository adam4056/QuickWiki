Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

1. Data Controller

QuickWiki is operated by its creator. Contact: support@thequickwiki.com.

2. Data Collected

  • Search queries: Not linked to identity, used to retrieve Wikipedia content.
  • LocalStorage: Stores cached summaries and search history on the user’s device only.
  • Analytics: We use Simple Analytics which collects anonymous statistics (no cookies, no personal data).
  • Server logs: Vercel may temporarily log IP addresses for security and performance; we don’t store or access them.

3. Purpose of Data Use

We use data only to operate and improve the Service. No personal data is stored or shared.

4. Analytics

We use Simple Analytics, a privacy-focused platform, to gather anonymous usage statistics. No cookies or tracking IDs are used.

5. LocalStorage

You can delete all local data from your browser anytime. Nothing is stored on our servers.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or any form of cross-session tracking. Therefore, no cookie consent banner is necessary.

7. Security

All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS. No personal data is processed or stored. We follow standard industry security practices.

8. User Rights

As no personal data is collected, GDPR rights such as access or deletion do not apply. LocalStorage can be cleared manually.

9. GDPR Compliance

The Service is designed to comply with GDPR. No identifiable personal data is collected or processed.

10. Changes to Policy

We may update this Policy occasionally. The current version is always available on this page.

11. Contact

For privacy-related concerns, email: support@thequickwiki.com
